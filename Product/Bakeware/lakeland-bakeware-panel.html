PROFESSIONAL NON-STICK

Our bakeware has a double layer of Quantum 2 non-stick both inside and out, which ensures excellent food release & makes them easy to clean too.

SURE-GRIP HANDLES

Lakeland added generous handles with sure-grip silicone pads to their bakeware to make getting them in and out of the oven easy.

NO ROLLED EDGES

The unrolled edges of our bakeware removes the risk of water traps and uncoated edges that might rust - making them completely dishwasher safe.
