Lakeland Bakeware

Bakeware made from heavy duty carbon steel with a double layer of Quantum 2 professional non-stick ensures your bakes will turn out perfectly every time.

What makes our bakeware different?

SURE-GRIP HANDLES

Lakeland added generous handles with sure-grip silicone pads to their bakewake to make getting them in and out of the oven easy.

NO ROLLED EDGES

The rolled edges found on most bake introduce a difficult to dry space underneath the rim, and the edges themselves can be difficult to coat. The unrolled edge design removes the risk of water traps and uncoated carbon steel edges that might rust - making them completely dishwasher safe.

FILL GUIDES

Fill line on Lakeland cake, loaf and bun tins show you the idea level of ingredients for a perfect, tin-filling rise and equally sized bakes.

PROFESSIONAL NON-STICK

Lakeland's bakeware has a double layer of professional-quality Quantum 2 non-stick both inside and out, which ensures excellent food release and makes them incredibly easy to clean too.

SPACE-SAVING STACKING

Many same-sized pieces nest inside each other and differently sized pieces can be stacked up to keep cupboards tidy.

SIZING GUIDES

Clear sizing guides on the cake tin bases in both cm and inches mean you can find the one you want at a glance.
