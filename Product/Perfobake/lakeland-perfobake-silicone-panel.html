MICRO-PERFORATIONS

Micro-perforations allow increased airflow so moisture can escape to achieve an even, crispy bake - no more soggy bottoms!

ADDITIONAL BENEFITS

Our silicone bakeware provides excellent heat conductivity & air circulation thanks to its micro-perforations. It's also microwave & dishwasher safe.

FLEXIBLE SILICONE

Non-stick, flexible silicone allows for easy food relase, cleaning, storage and rapid cooling.
