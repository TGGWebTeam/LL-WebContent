Lakeland Ovenware

Ovenware made from heavy duty carbon steel with a double layer of Quantum 2 professional non-stick to ensure excellent food release and easy cleaning every time.

Discover the unique features of our ovenware

TEXTURED BASE

The textured base on our roasting pans lifts your meat for healthier roasting and a crispier finish, and lets juices collect in the bottom for a delicious gravy.

SAVE-SPACING STACKING

Many same-sized pieces nest inside each other and differently sized pieces can be stacked up to keep cupboards tidy.

SURE GRIP HANDLES

We've added generous handles with sure-grip silicone pads to our ovenware to make getting them in and out of the oven easy.

POURING UP

The handy corner lip is great for collecting pan juices from your roast to make a delicious, flavourful gravy.

PROFESSIONAL NON-STICK

Lakeland's ovenware has a double layer of professional-quality Quantum 2 non-stick both inside and out, which ensures excellent food release and makes them incredibly easy to clean too.
