You can cook a whole chicken in only 20 minutes!

  1. Place stock, seasoning & chicken in steam case.
  2. Microwave on high for 10 minutes.
  3. Carefully turn over chicken & season
  4. Microwave for a further 10 minutes & enjoy!

Did you know?

Steaming is one of the healthiest cooking methods, preparing food with steam as well as in its own juice retains nutrients and flavour.
